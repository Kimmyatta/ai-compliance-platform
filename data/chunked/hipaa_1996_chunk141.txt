Complaints to the Covered Entity a. A Covered Entity must provide a process for individuals to make complaints concerning its policies and procedures or its compliance with such policies and procedures or the requirements of these regulations. b. A Covered Entity must document all complaints received and their disposition, if any. 5. Sanctions a. A Covered Entity must have and apply appropriate sanctions against members of its workforce who fail to comply with the policies and procedures or the requirements of the regulations. This standard does not apply to members of the workforce who are whistleblowers, victims of crime or who have filed a complaint, participated in an investigation or opposed an unlawful act or practice. b. A Covered Entity must document any sanctions that are applied. 6.
