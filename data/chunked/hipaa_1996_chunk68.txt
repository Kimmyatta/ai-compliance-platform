a. Pursuant to Process - A Covered Entity may disclose PHI as required by law, including laws that require reporting of certain types of wounds or injuries, except for laws regarding abuse, neglect and domestic violence subject to sections 2 and 3 above; or in compliance with and limited by: i. A court order, warrant, subpoena or summons issued by a judge; ii. A grand jury subpoena; iii. An administrative request, subpoena, summons, a civil investigative demand, or similar process authorized by law, provided that the information sought is relevant and material to a legitimate law enforcement inquiry, the request is specific and limited in scope, and de-identified information could not reasonably be used.
