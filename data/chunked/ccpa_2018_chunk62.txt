Any provision of a contract or agreement of any kind that purports to waive or limit in any way this subdivision shall be void and unenforceable. 1798.140. Definitions For purposes of this title: (a) “Advertising and marketing” means a communication by a business or a person acting on the business’ behalf in any medium intended to induce a consumer to obtain goods, services, or employment.
