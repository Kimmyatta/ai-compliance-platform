B. Uses and Disclosures for Which an Authorization or Opportunity to Agree or Object is not Required A Covered Entity may use or disclose PHI without the written authorization of the individual or the opportunity for the individual to agree or object in the situations described below. When the Covered Entity is required to inform the individual of, or the individual may agree to, a use or disclosure permitted below, the Covered Entity’s information and the individual’s agreement may be given orally. 1. Uses and Disclosures Required by Law b.
