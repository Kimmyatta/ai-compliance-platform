Family Members A Covered Entity may, in accordance with paragraphs (a) or (b) below, disclose to a family member, other relative or close personal friend of the individual, or any other person identified by the individual, the PHI directly relevant to such person’s involvement with the individual’s care or payment for that care. A Covered Entity may, in accordance with paragraphs (a), (b) or (c) below, disclose PHI to notify or assist in notifying a family member, personal representative or other person responsible for the care of the individual of the individual’s location, general condition or death.
