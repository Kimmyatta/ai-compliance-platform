Health information means any information, whether oral or recorded in any form or medium, that: 1) Is created or received by a health care provider, health plan, public health authority, employer, life insurer, school or university, or health care clearinghouse; and 2) Relates to the past, present, or future physical or mental health or condition of an 11 individual; the provision of health care to an individual; or the past, present, or future payment for the provision of health care to an individual.
