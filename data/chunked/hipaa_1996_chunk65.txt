b. Satisfactory Assurances of Notice - Regarding item (a)(ii)(A) above, a Covered Entity receives satisfactory assurances from a party seeking PHI if the Covered Entity receives from such party a written statement and documentation that: i. The party has made a good faith attempt to provide written notice to the individual; ii. The notice included sufficient information about the litigation or proceeding in which the PHI is requested to permit the individual to raise an objection in the court or tribunal; and iii. The time for filing objections has lapsed and no objections were filed or all objections that were raised have been resolved by the court and disclosure is consistent with such resolution.
