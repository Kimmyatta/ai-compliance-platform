A Covered Entity must have in place appropriate administrative, technical and physical safeguards to protect the privacy of PHI. b. A Covered Entity must reasonably safeguard PHI from any intentional or unintentional use or disclosure that is in violation of the standards, implementation specifications or other requirements of the regulations. c.
