Fees - If the individual requests a copy of the PHI or agrees to a summary or explanation, the Covered Entity may impose a reasonable, cost-based fee, provided the fee includes only the cost of: i. Copying, including the cost of supplies and labor; ii. Postage, when the individual has requested the copy be mailed; and iii. Preparing an explanation or summary of the PHI, if agreed to by the individual. 4.
