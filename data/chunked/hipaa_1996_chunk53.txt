If the individual is present or otherwise available prior to the use or disclosure permitted above and has the capacity to make health care decisions, the Covered Entity may use or disclose the PHI if it obtain’s the individual’s agreement, it provides the individual with the opportunity to object and the individual does not express an objection, or it reasonably infers from the circumstances that the individual does not object. b.
