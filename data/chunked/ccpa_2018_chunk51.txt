Methods of Limiting Sale, Sharing, and Use of Personal Information and Use of Sensitive Personal Information (a) A business that sells or shares consumers’ personal information or uses or discloses consumers’ sensitive personal information for purposes other than those authorized by subdivision (a) of Section 1798.121 shall, in a form that is reasonably accessible to consumers: Page 15 of 65 (1) Provide a clear and conspicuous link on the business’ internet homepages, titled “Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information,” to an internet web page that enables a consumer, or a person authorized by the consumer, to opt out of the sale or sharing of the consumer’s personal information.
