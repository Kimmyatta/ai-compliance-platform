A Covered Entity participating in an OHCA that performs a function, activity or service as described in (i) or (ii) above, does not because of that performance become a business associate of the other covered entities in the OHCA. A Covered Entity may be a Business Associate of another Covered Entity. When conducting a covered transaction, a Covered Entity must use the applicable medical data code sets as specified in the implementation specifications adopted by DHHS that are valid at the time the health care is furnished and the nonmedical data code sets as specified in the implementation specifications adopted by DHHS that are valid at the time the transaction is initiated. Code set means any set of codes used to encode data elements, such as tables of terms, medical concepts, medical diagnostic codes, or medical procedure codes. It includes the codes and descriptors of the codes.
