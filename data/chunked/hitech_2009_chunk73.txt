Each year, the National Coordinator must provide a report to Congress summarizing the annual reports submitted by grantees. Awards are not permitted before January 1, 2010. Clinical Education The Act authorizes the Secretary to create a demonstration program for awarding Demonstration competitive grants to medical, dental, and nursing schools, and to other graduate health education programs to integrate HIT into the clinical education of health care professionals. To be eligible, grantees must submit a strategic plan. A grant may not cover more than 50% of the costs of any activity for which assistance is provided, though the Secretary has the authority to waive that cost-sharing requirement. The Secretary annually must report to designated House and Senate Committees on the demonstrations, with recommendations.
