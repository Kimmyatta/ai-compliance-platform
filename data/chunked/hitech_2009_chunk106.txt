To avoid duplication of payments, if a physician is both an MA-affiliated provider and eligible for the maximum incentive payment under the fee- for-service (FFS) program, then the payment is to be made only under the FFS program. If the physician is eligible for less than the maximum incentive payment, then the payment is to be made only to the MA organization.
