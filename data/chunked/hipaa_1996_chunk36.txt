The group health plan, or a health insurance issuer or HMO with respect to the group health plan, may disclose to the plan sponsor information on whether an individual is participating in the group health plan, or is enrolled in or has disenrolled from a health insurance issuer or HMO offered by the plan. Plan documents must be amended to incorporate provisions which establish the permitted uses and disclosures of such information by the plan sponsor, must provide that the group health plan will disclose PHI to the plan sponsor only upon receipt of a certification from the plan sponsor that the plan documents have been amended to incorporate the provisions required by the Privacy regulations, and must provide for adequate separation between the group health plan and the plan sponsor.
