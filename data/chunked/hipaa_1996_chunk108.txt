Provision of Notice - A Covered Entity must make the notice available on request to any person and to individuals, as follows: a. Health Plans - A health plan must provide notice: i. No later than the compliance date for the health plan to individuals then covered by the plan; ii. Thereafter, at the time of enrollment for new enrollees; and iii. Within 60 days of a material revision to the notice to individuals then covered by the plan. iv. No less frequently than once every three years, the health plan must notify individuals then covered by the plan of the availability of the notice and how to obtain the notice. v. The health plan satisfies the notice requirements of this subsection if notice is provided to the named insured of a policy under which coverage is provided to the named insured and one or more dependents. vi.
