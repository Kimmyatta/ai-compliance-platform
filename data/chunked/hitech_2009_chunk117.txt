Eligible pediatricians may receive up to two-thirds of those amounts. Allowable costs for children's hospitals and acute-care hospitals are based on the Medicare EHR incentive payment formula, with some modifications. Hospital EHR technology payments may not be made after 2016, unless the provider received a payment for the previous year, and may not be made over a period of more than six years.
