Valid Authorizations A valid authorization must contain the following elements: b. A specific and meaningful description of the information to be used or disclosed; c. The name or other specific identification of the person(s) authorized to make the requested use or disclosure; d. The name or other specific identification of the person(s) to whom the Covered Entity may make the requested use or disclosure; e. A description of each purpose of the requested use or disclosure; f. An expiration date or event that relates to the individual or the purpose of the use or disclosure. The statement “end of research study,” “none,” or similar language is sufficient if the authorization is for a use or disclosure of PHI for research; g. Signature of the individual and date.
