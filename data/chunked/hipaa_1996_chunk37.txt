Multiple Covered Functions A Covered Entity that performs multiple covered functions that would make the entity any combination of a health plan, a covered health care provider, and a health care clearinghouse, must comply with the Privacy regulations as applicable to the health plan, health care provider, or health care clearinghouse covered functions performed. A Covered Entity that performs multiple covered functions may use or disclose PHI of individuals who receive the Covered Entity’s health plan or health care provider services, but not both, only for the purposes related to the appropriate function being performed.
