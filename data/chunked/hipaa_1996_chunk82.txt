Uses and Disclosures for Specialized Government Functions a. Armed Forces Personnel - A Covered Entity may use and disclose PHI of individuals who are Armed Forces personnel for activities deemed necessary by appropriate military command to assure proper execution of the military mission, if the appropriate military authority has published in the Federal Register the following information: i. Appropriate military command authorities; and ii. The purposes for which the PHI may be used or disclosed. b. Separation or Discharge - A Covered Entity that is a component of the Departments of Defense or Transportation may disclose to the Department of Veterans Affairs (DVA) the PHI of an individual who is a member of the Armed Forces upon the separation or discharge of the individual from military service for the purpose of determining eligibility for DVA benefits.
