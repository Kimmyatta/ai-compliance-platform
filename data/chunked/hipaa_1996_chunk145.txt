Nothing in this paragraph may be used to excuse a failure to comply with the law. d. Changes to Privacy Practices Stated in the Notice i. To implement a change in its policies and procedures to reflect a change in a privacy practice that is stated in its notice, a Covered Entity must: A. Ensure that the policy or procedure, as revised to reflect a change in a privacy practice that is stated in its notice, complies with the regulations; B. Document the policy or procedure, as revised, as required by subsection 10; and C. Revise the notice as required to state the changed practice and make the revised notice available as 56 required. The Covered Entity may not implement a change to a policy or procedure prior to the effective date of the revised notice. ii.
