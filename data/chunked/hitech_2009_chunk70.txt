State Planning and The National Coordinator is authorized to award planning and implementation Implementation Grants grants to states or qualified state-designated entities to facilitate and expand CRS-15 Topic Summary of Provision Current Requirements and Activities electronic health information exchanges. To qualify as a state-designated entity, an entity must be a nonprofit organization with broad stakeholder representation on its governing board and adopt nondiscrimination and conflict-of-interest policies.
