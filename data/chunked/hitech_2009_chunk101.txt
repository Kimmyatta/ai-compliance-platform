Covered entities may, but are not required, to obtain an individual's general consent to use or disclose PHI for treatment, payment, or health care operations. Psychotherapy notes (i.e., notes recorded by mental health professionals during counseling) are afforded special protection under the privacy rule. Almost all uses and disclosures of such information require patient authorization.
