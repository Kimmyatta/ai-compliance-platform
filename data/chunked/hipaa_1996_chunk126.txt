b. Timely Action by the Covered Entity - The Covered Entity must act on a request for amendment no later than 60 days after receipt of the request, as follows: i. If it grants the request, in whole or in part, it must take the actions required by section (3) below. ii. If it denies the requested amendment, in whole or in part, it 49 must provide a written denial. iii. If it is unable to act within the time required, the Covered Entity may extend the time for such action by no more than 30 days, provided that it provides a written statement to the individual within the original time limit stating the reasons for the delay and the date by which it will complete its action, and the Covered Entity may have only one such extension. 3.
