Uses and Disclosures for Health Oversight Activities A Covered Entity may disclose PHI to a health oversight agency for oversight activities authorized by law, including audits, investigations, inspections, licensure or disciplinary actions, civil, administrative or criminal actions or other activities necessary for oversight of: b. The health care system c. Government benefit programs for which health information is relevant to beneficiary eligibility d. Entities subject to government regulatory programs for which health information is necessary for determining compliance, or e. Entities subject to civil rights laws for which health information is necessary for determining compliance.
