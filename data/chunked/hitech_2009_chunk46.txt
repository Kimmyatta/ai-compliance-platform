HITECH Act: Explanation of Provisions Lawmakers incorporated the HITECH Act in the American Recovery and Reinvestment Act of 2009 (ARRA; H.R. 1, H.Rept. 111-16), the economic stimulus bill that the President signed into law on February 17, 2009 (P.L. 111-5). The HITECH Act is an amalgam of the two House bills Congressional Research Service 9 from the 110th Congress. It contains three sets of provisions that are expected to boost HIT adoption among health care providers in the coming years.
